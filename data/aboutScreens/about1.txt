Story

In the year 2149, giant asteroid fields are
revealed in the far reaches of space. But 
these are no ordinary fields, they are highly
 concentrated with crystals of incredible 
rarity and value. Suddenly, untold wealth is
 up for grabs - that is, for those who dare 
venture to it. Thousands of the bravest 
pilots, most remorseless pirates, and most 
powerful corporations blast off to vie for it.
