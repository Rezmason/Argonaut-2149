Story

The most ambitious individuals call themselves
 Argonauts, those on a quest for their own 
"golden fleece" of sorts. Their ship of choice
 is the ZX-24, a single thruster fighter 
capable of housing plasma blasters as well as 
arrays of missiles, bombs, and other exotic
 technology to fend off pirates and other
agressors. The ship's favor among Argonauts 
has deemed it the "Argo".
